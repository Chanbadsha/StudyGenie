# StudyGenie

> An AI-powered study companion that helps students create, organize, and understand learning materials through intelligent content generation, personalized recommendations, and an interactive AI tutor.

---

## 📖 Overview

StudyGenie is a production-ready full-stack Agentic AI application built with **Next.js, Express.js, TypeScript, MongoDB, and Google Gemini**.

The platform enables students to organize study materials, generate AI-powered notes and quizzes, chat with an intelligent tutor, and monitor their learning progress through an interactive dashboard.

Unlike traditional AI chat applications, StudyGenie is context-aware. It understands users' study materials, previous AI conversations, and learning history to provide more personalized assistance.
